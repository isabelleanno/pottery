Our Products

At Seize The Clay Pottery, we sell a wide variety of high-quality tools, and sell only the best responsibly sourced clay. Below you will see a short description of the types of products we sell, and a table listing each product type and the cost.


Tools

All of our tools were hand-made
by local woodworkers. We are so
confident in the quality that we
offer have a year long warranty-
if your tool breaks within a year,
we’ll replace it for free!

Clay

We mostly sell different types of
earthenware clay. Stoneware and
porcelain are available within a few
days- you can stop in to pick up a
pound, but you must order more than
5 lb. Call or place an order in person.
